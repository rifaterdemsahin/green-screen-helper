Sony ZV-1 Sample Settings

These are recommended starting points for a Sony ZV-1 camera for green screen recording.

  • Mode: Manual (M)
  • Aperture: f/2.8 or lower (to blur the background)
  • ISO: 100-400 (as low as possible)
  • Shutter Speed: 1/60 (for 30fps) or 1/50 (for 24fps)
  • Picture Profile: PP8 (S-Log3) or Cine4. If using S-Log, you will need to color grade your footage. For a simpler workflow, use a more standard profile with saturation slightly increased.
  • Focus: Manual Focus (MF)
  • White Balance: Custom, set to your lighting.
