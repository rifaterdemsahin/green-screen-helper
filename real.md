# Project OKRs: Green Screen Helper

This document outlines the Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) that define the success of the Green Screen Helper project.

---

### Objective 1: Empower creators with a simple, effective tool for perfect green screen setup.

*   **Key Result 1:** Provide real-time, intuitive feedback on screen evenness, allowing users to adjust their lighting and camera settings instantly.
*   **Key Result 2:** Reduce the time and guesswork involved in chroma key setup by offering precise color picking and tolerance controls.
*   **Key Result 3:** Achieve a high user satisfaction rate, measured by positive feedback and community adoption.

---

### Objective 2: Deliver a comprehensive and user-friendly learning resource for green screen techniques.

*   **Key Result 1:** Consolidate essential knowledge into easy-to-access guides (Shooting, Camera, and Calculator) that are available directly within the application.
*   **Key Result 2:** The guides are utilized by over 70% of the app's users, indicating their value and effectiveness.
*   **Key Result 3:** The Room Layout Calculator helps users successfully plan and visualize their physical space, preventing common setup issues before they happen.

---

### Objective 3: Build a polished, responsive, and highly shareable web application.

*   **Key Result 1:** Achieve a 95+ score on Google Lighthouse for Performance, Accessibility, and SEO, ensuring a high-quality user experience.
*   **Key Result 2:** Ensure the application is fully responsive and functional across all major web browsers and device sizes (desktop, tablet, and mobile).
*   **Key Result 3:** Foster community growth by integrating clear calls-to-action and social sharing links, leading to a 20% month-over-month increase in users.
